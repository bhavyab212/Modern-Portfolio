LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Please add the following logo files to this directory (public/logos/):

1. kv1.png - Kendriya Vidyalaya logo for LIGHT MODE
2. kv2.png - Kendriya Vidyalaya logo for DARK MODE  
3. mdu.png - Maharshi Dayanand University logo
4. iitm.png - IIT Madras logo

REQUIREMENTS:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: Square images (recommended 200x200px or higher)
- The logos will be displayed in white circular containers
- Make sure logos are clear and high quality

The component will automatically:
- Switch between kv1.png and kv2.png based on light/dark theme
- Display all logos with white circular backgrounds
- Apply neon glow effects to institution names
